The ESMH thinks of itself as a center for the exploration of expressionist art after 1945. The museum’s exhibition program will present the artist Emil Schumacher in relation to parallel international developments in the arts and his chronological surroundings. Moreover, young artists, whose works are close to Schumacher’s œuvre in form or content will be shown in regular exhibits.
