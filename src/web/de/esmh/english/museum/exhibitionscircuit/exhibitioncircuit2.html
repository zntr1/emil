Studio
The tour starts with the studio on the ground floor. In order to show visitors Schumacher’s techniques and methods, his studio in Hagen was transferred to the museum and serves as a documentary introduction to Emil Schumacher's life and œuvre. As documentation space, the studio at the bottom of the stairs introduces Emil Schumacher’s life and work. Next to his illustrated biography, film sequences show the artist at work and provide an introduction to his œuvre.
