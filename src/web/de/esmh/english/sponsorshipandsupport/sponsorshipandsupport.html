SPONSORSHIP AND SUPPORT
The Emil Schumacher Museum was established with the support of many generous and active sponsors. A lively museum owes ist success to the commitment of private persons as well as corporations. The Emil Schumacher Museum sees itself as a house for Hagen and its citizens, as representational space that presents guests with the cultural tradition of Hagen. We realize that this commitment contributes to the continuing success of the museum. We greatly appreciate the support from all of our sponsors.
